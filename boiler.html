Boilers – Operation & Maintenance

We provide specialized services for the operation, maintenance, and overhauling of boilers of all types, including IBR and non-IBR units. Our services ensure safety, efficiency, and compliance with statutory requirements. With skilled operators, technicians, and supervisors, we deliver reliable boiler management solutions with strict adherence to safety standards and schedules.

Services

  1. Operation & Maintenance of all types of Boilers (IBR & Non-IBR).
  2. Annual maintenance contracts (AMC) for Boilers.
  3. Boiler start-up, shutdown, and troubleshooting.
  4. Efficiency improvement & energy audit support.
  5. Cleaning & inspection of boiler tubes and accessories.
  6. Maintenance of steam pipelines, valves, and fittings.
  7. Overhauling of Thermo packs and auxiliaries.
  8. Statutory compliance support & certification assistance.

Technical Manpower Supply (Skilled & unskilled)

  1. Boiler Operators (IBR Certified).
  2. Boiler Firemen.
  3. Boiler Technicians (ITI/DEG).
  4. Welders & Fitters (Boiler pipelines & structures).
  5. Instrumentation Technicians (Boiler controls & safety devices).
  6. Helpers for Boiler Operation & Maintenance.
  7. Energy Auditors & Safety Supervisors.
  8. Diploma & B.Tech Engineers (Mechanical/Instrumentation).
