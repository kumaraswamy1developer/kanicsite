Water Treatment Plant – Operation & Maintenance

We provide specialized services for the operation, maintenance, and servicing of water treatment plants (WTP, ETP, STP, and RO systems), ensuring safe, efficient, and reliable treatment of water and wastewater. Our expert team focuses on preventive and corrective maintenance, improving system life, compliance with standards, and sustainable operations.

Services

  1. Operation & Maintenance of Water Treatment Plants (WTP, ETP, STP, RO).
  2. Preventive maintenance of pumps, motors, membranes, and filters.
  3. Overhauling of clarifiers, aerators, and dosing systems.
  4. Cleaning, inspection, and replacement of sand, carbon, and micron filters.
  5. Monitoring and adjustment of water quality parameters (pH, TDS, hardness, turbidity).
  6. Chemical dosing system calibration and management.
  7. Troubleshooting and repair of pipelines, valves, and control panels.
  8. Energy optimization and performance tracking of WTP systems.
  9. Installation & commissioning support for new plants.
  10. Annual maintenance contracts (AMC).

Technical Manpower Supply (Skilled & unskilled)

  1. Water Treatment Plant Operators (RO/STP/ETP certified).
  2. Process Engineers for WTP/ETP/STP.
  3. Chemists & Lab Technicians (Water Quality Testing).
  4. Mechanical Technicians (pumps, motors, pipelines).
  5. Electrical Technicians (control panels & motors).
  6. Helpers for plant operation & maintenance.
  7. Instrumentation Technicians (flow meters, sensors, SCADA).
  8. Diploma & B.Tech Engineers (Chemical, Environmental, Mechanical, Electrical).
