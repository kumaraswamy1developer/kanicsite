About

KANIC Engineering Services has been a “one-stop” solution provider for diverse facility services since 2008. From industrial technical staffing and general housekeeping to specialized utility and engineering solutions, we follow a start-to-finish approach and deliver tailored, end-to-end services that meet customer requirements.

With vast expertise in industrial utilities, HVAC systems, electrical systems, and machine spares, our team is guided by professionals and powered by a dedicated workforce. We pride ourselves on tackling challenging projects and minimizing equipment downtime through the support of our dynamic engineers.

MISSION

With our team expertise in managing Pharma companies, Hospitals, Milk dairies, Residential and commercial Buildings, We believe in “Single point of Contract” approach for the provision of integrated Facility management solutions through our management initiatives and property evaluation methodology.
